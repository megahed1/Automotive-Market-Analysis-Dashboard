# 🚗 Automotive Market Analysis & Campaign Strategy Dashboard

![Overview Dashboard](https://github.com/megahed1/Automotive-Market-Analysis-Dashboard/blob/main/Dashboard/Overview.png?raw=true)

## 📌 Project Overview
A car dealership owner is planning a major upcoming marketing campaign. To maximize the Return on Investment (ROI), this Power BI project was developed to analyze historical car sales data, understand customer demographics, and identify which car models and engine types generate the highest profitability. 

The insights derived from this dashboard will directly dictate the marketing budget allocation, target audience, and timing of the campaign.

## 📁 Repository Structure
Here is a breakdown of the files and folders included in this repository:

- 📂 **`Dashboard/`**: Contains high-resolution screenshots of the Power BI dashboards (Overview & Engine Impact).
- 📂 **`Icons/`**: Contains the custom UI icons used to design the dashboard layout.
- 📄 **`Car Dashboard.pbix`**: The complete, interactive Power BI project file (contains Data Modeling, DAX measures, and Visualizations).
- 📄 **`car sales.csv`**: The raw dataset used for the analysis.
- 📄 **`README.md`**: Project documentation and key findings.

## 🎯 Business Problem & Objectives
The client needed actionable, data-driven answers to the following questions to shape their campaign:
1. Who is our primary customer base (Gender & Income)?
2. When is the optimal time to launch the marketing campaign?
3. Which car brands and specific models bring in the most revenue?
4. How does the engine type (e.g., OHC vs. DOHC) impact sales performance?

## 📊 Key Insights & Campaign Recommendations

### 1. Target Audience (Demographics)
- **Insight:** Males account for a massive **78.49%** of total revenue. Furthermore, the average annual income of buyers is relatively high (~\$830K).
- **Recommendation:** The marketing messaging, visuals, and ad placements should be heavily targeted toward high-income male demographics.

### 2. Campaign Timing (Seasonality)
- **Insight:** The line chart reveals a massive spike in sales starting in **September and peaking through December** (Q4).
- **Recommendation:** The dealership should allocate the majority of its advertising budget to the fourth quarter to capture this seasonal demand.

### 3. Top Performing Brands & Models
- **Insight:** **Chevrolet, Ford, and Dodge** are the top three revenue-generating companies. The best-selling specific models include the **Jetta, Continental, Montero Sport, and LS400**.
- **Recommendation:** These specific brands and models should be the "hero" products featured in the upcoming campaign advertisements.

### 4. Engine Type Profitability
- **Insight:** Vehicles equipped with **Double Overhead Camshaft (DOHC)** engines are more profitable, accounting for **52.88%** of engine-related revenue compared to standard Overhead Camshafts.
- **Recommendation:** Highlight the performance and availability of DOHC engines in the marketing copy, as this is a clear preference for buyers.
